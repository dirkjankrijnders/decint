Specification Decoder interface
===============================

Sources of signal
-----------------

- Railsync on loconet connector (isolated)
- Loconet
- Track (isolated)

Sources of power
----------------
Linear power regulator
- External
- Track (Only with track as signal source)

Feedback
--------
All Isolated

- Ack generator
- 4 feedback ports

ISP
---
On decoder board itself, or via Arduino bootloader (USB/FTDI).
